Mono Acting

Description :

Does the sight of the stage send a flutter through your heart? Do you spend hours in front of the mirror practicing Henry the fifth? Well, look no further, because this is the event for you!

Rules :

  • The performance should be anywhere from 2 to 5 minutes long.
  • The primary language of the monologue should be English.
  • A table and a chair will be provided; other props must be sourced by the participant.
  • Use of profanity is to be kept to a minimum.
  • The judge's decision will be final and binding.
